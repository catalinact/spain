What to Eat

Try the Churros con chocolate! You may be familiar with Mexican churros, but though both are fried, they are not the same thing.
Spanish churros are a plainer fried dough typically served with a cup of thick, rich hot chocolate to dip the churros in.

What to Do

Visit the Real Alcazar de Sevilla. A royal palace built for Moorish kings, this popular site
is an incredible look into the history of Sevilla, and just an incredible, luxurious site to behold!
